Kinds of Plastic

Bottles

Non-reusable plastic bottles, such as water bottles, soda bottles, etc.

Utensils

Any plastic utensils, such as forks, spoons, knives, etc.

Bags

Any non-reusable plastic bags, such as grocery bags and Ziploc baggies.

Containers

Any plastic containers, such as Tupperware or any food storage container. Can also include packaging for various items.

Other

Any non-reusable plastic product that does not fall into the above four categories, such as bubble wrap, plastic plates, packaging/wrappers, etc.
